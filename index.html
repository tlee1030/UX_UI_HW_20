Bootstrap Portfolio Case Study

This is my design process from start to finish

Step 1: Choosing a Business or Cause I Believe In

It is important to start a project with purpose and knowing what you are trying to fix, redesign or design. In my case, I always look for projects specific to my interests so it keeps my interest. So what is it I believe in most?

Step 2: Research what will make it easier for me to travel the world on a budget.

Obviously, traveling the world can be a daunting task when you don't have a plan and you are on a budget. That is why I needed to research the easiest way for me to travel the world with the tight budget I have, while still being able to enjoy activities, restaurants and special accommodations. In my research I realized while there are different travel booking services to help arrange my travel plans, there is nothing comprehensive to help me book everything from my flights down to my restaurant reservations on a set budget, which I can adjust for the days I'm on vacation. Therefore, I created ZipTrip, an app that books everything according to your budget.
